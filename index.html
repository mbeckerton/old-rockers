About Us

We are a 1960’s rock band called Old rockers.
We have over 50 years of experience of live performing.
We do weddings, parties, charity events, in fact pretty much anything we’re required for.
You can request the type of music you’d like to hear or you can leave it for us to provide you with some random hits.
We travel all the UK so distance is not an issue.
We use the latest equipment to provide that real live concert feel.

We do weddings

Want us for you special day?
We cover a wide range of songs from slow love songs to more upbeat hits to make your wedding a day to remember. We tailor our music to suit your needs. Guaranteed to keep your guests dancing all night long.
